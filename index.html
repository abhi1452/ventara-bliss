Welcome to Ventara Bliss

A villa built for
slow mornings.

Tucked into the folds of the Sahyadris in Karla, a few quiet kilometres from the crowds of central Lonavala, Ventara Bliss is a four-bedroom private pool villa designed for the way you actually want to spend a weekend — unhurried, barefoot and entirely your own.

Ventara takes its name from the idea of an opening — a window, a threshold, a way through. That instinct runs through the villa: glass doors that fold the balcony into the bedroom, a pool framed by nothing but a low white wall and the hills beyond it. This is not a resort built at scale. It is one house, one pool, one address in Karla.
